I'm Harivarshan K A, currently pursuing M.Tech in Green Energy Technology as an SAF Scholar at Pondicherry University after earning my B.Tech in Civil Engineering with a CGPA of 8.06 from Puducherry Technological University. I'm also the founder of harrywhite, a design firm dedicated to making high-quality, exceptional design accessible to everyone, and a founding member of UNITY Foundation, which focuses on empowering youth and building stronger communities. I enjoy working on ideas that bring together technology, design, and sustainability to make a positive impact.
